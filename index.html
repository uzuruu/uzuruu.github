Sam Sam

a manifesto


We propose:
  1. To use our lives as platforms for art making. We aim to merge our work with the everyday. At times the process and the result are the same.
  2. We wish to further develop and unfold our own collaborative working partnership and the possibilities that it holds.
  3. Is it possible to make art collaboratively whilst maintaining a sense of self? Can a collective spirit of individuality be achieved?
  4. We pay respect to artists such as Gilbert and George and Fischli and Weiss. They are artists that grapple with themselves in the everyday environment that they live in. They disrupt/devalue hierarchies found in contemporary conventions in the art world and beyond, and play with their stigma.

Emily & Tom
